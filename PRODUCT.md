# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individual dream archivists and introspective users who capture their subconscious narratives. They typically use external AI tools (like voice transcription and image generation) and need a private, sophisticated "harness" to organize, store, and revisit these experiences.

## Product Purpose

A client-side, privacy-first archive system for preserving the narrative of dreams. It serves as the long-term storage and retrieval interface for structured dream data, transforming raw captures into a curated, visually rich library.

## Brand Personality

Introspective, sophisticated, ethereal.

- The voice is poetic and calm, not clinical.
- The tone is observant and respectful of the subconscious.
- Success is felt through a seamless, cinematic recording and viewing experience.

## Strategic Design Principles

1. **Glass-First Depth**: Use layered glassmorphism to create a sense of looking "through" the interface into the subconscious, avoiding flat surfaces where possible.
2. **Narrative Focus**: Content (stories, fragments, and imagery) takes precedence over UI chrome.
3. **Atmospheric Contrast**: Deep navy foundations with high-contrast, ethereal accents to mirror the shift from waking life to dream state.
4. **Cinematic Utility**: Every workflow (adding an entry, browsing the gallery) should feel like interacting with a sophisticated archive, not a generic database.

## Anti-references

- **Clinical/Scientific Dashboards**: No charts, no heavy "data visualization," no sterile white backgrounds.
- **Generic SaaS Templates**: Avoid standard cards, side-stripes, and modal-heavy flows.
- **Social Media Patterns**: No "likes," no public sharing buttons, no feed-style clutter.
